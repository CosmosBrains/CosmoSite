TerraSphere

The TerraSphere is a massive artificial universe constructed by some sort of ancient race, classified “Pridem.” It houses eight planets and various other bodies, as well as the sun. Earth is one of the only planets in the solar system to support life, and is the homeworld of humanity.

The TerraSphere remains mysterious in that its history, purpose, and mechanisms remain unknown. Furthermore, there are various phenomena and creatures that the laws of reason fail to explain (Ghosts, Ghost Worlds, Birch Trees, ‘Unfinished’ Rooms, etc). There also exists a phenomenon known as “The Red Moon,” Which is believed to be some sort of deity, as evidenced by worship by Northern African Civilizations. Currently, it is believed to be in control of a terrorist group known as “The Liberators,” whose influence is beyond what was once thought possible by such small groups. To counter these anomalies, the United States of America has formed the CRP (Chaos Removal Platform), a branch between police and military that handles anomalies activity across the world. The UN also has its own organizations to handle anomalies, such as the UNAC (United Nations Anomaly Containment).

It is understood that the entire structure was constructed (as mentioned) by a god-like “super” race that dominated the entire multiverse that has since disappeared (Research has linked this disappearance to a massive interuniversal war beyond that of human comprehension, according to the remaining Pridem War Earth Class War Machines littered across Earth, as well as other aspects of the artificial universe). Unfortunately, exploration beyond that of the solar system is impossible, as there exists a shell that surrounds the entire solar system. It is nearly impossible to destroy (no such methods have been found) and entry and exit systems seem to have been locked down. Therefore, exploration beyond the TerraSphere is VERY limited. It is understood that beyond the TerraSphere is a space known as “The Void,” which exists in this space there exists other universes. This “Void” can be compared to a “bulk” in terms of Multiverse Theory.
